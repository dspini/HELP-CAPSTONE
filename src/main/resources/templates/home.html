We're Here to HELP

The Household Emergency Logistics Planner (HELP) is an application for planning and organizing an emergency response plan for a single household and family groups.


Meet Our Team

Just a Few Steps Away from Your Emergency Plan


Register and Create Your Group

Register as your group's administrator, name your group and set an emergency meetup location.

Invite Members to Join Your Group

Invite members of your household to join your group and have access to your family's emergency plan.

Create your Checklist

Set a list of sentimental items and save links to important documents and websites that can quickly be accessed during an emergency.

Have Your Plan On-The-Go

Access your plan, view your checklist and send messages to your family members on the go from your mobile device.
